Intersting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they can use to wrap around tree branches

Intersting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredible complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intersting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy Bears are stuffed toys in the form of a bear
  • Teddy Bears were named in the honor of President Theodore Roosevelt
  • The incident of Roosevelt sparing the bear led to the popularity of Teddy Bears
